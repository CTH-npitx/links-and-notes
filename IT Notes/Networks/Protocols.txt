IP Protocol (#RAS Syndrome): the most basic network protocol, it is responsible for routing data packets across a network.

TCP (Transmission Control Protocol): a guaranteed delivery protocol, ensures all data arrives without error, it will re-request missing info
UDP (User Datagram Protocol): just sends all data, doesn't double check. typically used for video or audio calls. If a little goes missing, eh, whatever
