peripherial devices add functionality that is not inherent to the computer (it will function with or without)
you don't need, say, a keyboard to run it

inputs:
microphones
keyboard
mouse
scanner
they take non-digital things, like sound, into binary

outputs:
they take the digital signal from an input, and convert it into, say, vibrations


storage devices
used to store stuff, practically necessary


communication
router: route multiple computers together
